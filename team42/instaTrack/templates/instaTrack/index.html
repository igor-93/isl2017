Welcome

Dandli is a simple application designed for professional Instagram users, to help keep track of your content and expand your network.

See Reactions

Easily check what impact your posts have, by looking at out performance visualizations. Quickly find important comments and respond immediately.

Personalized Recommendations

Want to know how to do things? Why not look at how other users are doing it? With our app, you can simply mark users, which share the same goal as you, and get recommendations for tags and timing.

Keep Your Network Clean

To grow your network, it is important to appear as a high quality content producer. This should also reflect in your interests. Therefore we will show you people how might not be worthy of your follow anymore, e.g. unpopular or inactive users.
